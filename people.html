Principal Investigator

Sergio Maldonado, Ph.D.

Lecturer (Assistant Professor)
Faculty of Engineering and Physical Sciences
University of Southampton
Southampton, UK

  • RG

Sergio Maldonado’s research interests include various aspects of Environmental Fluid Mechanics, generally falling in the following two categories: particle-fluid interactions (e.g. algae cells in flows, flexible fibres in turbulence, spheres in a boundary layer) and the development of Physics Informed Neural Networks (which may represent an attractive alternative to CFD solvers).

Trajectory:

  • 2017-present. Lecturer (Assistant Professor), University of Southampton, UK.
  • 2025-present. Visiting Faculty, Tecnológico de Monterrey, Mexico.
  • 2016-2017. Postdoctoral Fellow, Stanford University, CA, USA.

Education:

  • 2016 PhD in Environmental Fluid Mechanics, University of Edinburgh, UK. (supervisor: Prof Alistair Borthwick)
  • 2011 MSc Hydraulic Engineering (cum laude), National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), Mexico.
  • 2009 BSc Mechanical Engineering, Tecnológico de Monterrey, Mexico.
